Samantha Dolin

Founder of Precision Quality Systems

Samantha Dolin founded Precision Quality Systems with a clear mission: to help businesses build efficient, compliant, and scalable systems that improve quality, optimize operations, and support long-term growth.

With a background in quality systems engineering, industrial engineering, process improvement, and data-driven decision making, Samantha brings a practical and results-focused approach to every engagement. Her experience includes developing and implementing quality management systems, managing compliance initiatives, improving operational workflows, and supporting continuous improvement across manufacturing environments.

Through Precision Quality Systems, she works closely with organizations to design customized solutions around their specific operations, goals, and challenges. Her approach combines quality systems development, operational excellence, digitization, and strategic improvement to help businesses reduce waste, improve efficiency, strengthen compliance, and increase profitability.

Samantha also has a strong research background, having published and conducted experiments with NASA as part of the Reduced Gravity Flight Opportunities Program. Her work included heat transfer and fluid mechanics experiments performed aboard zero-gravity flights, giving her a deep foundation in data analysis, problem-solving, and technical design.
